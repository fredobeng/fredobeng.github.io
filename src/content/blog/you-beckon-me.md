You blessed my senses.  
Little though I was,  
You showed me You.  
And now, You beckon me.  
  
I don't know what You see.  
Your ways are higher than mine,  
And Your thoughts are too.  

Just as I am, I come.   
What shall I offer You?  
My heart, my strength, my mind?  
All of me I give.
